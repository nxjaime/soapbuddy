## ‚ùì **Slide 15: Risk Factors**

### Transparent Assessment of Key Risks

**Market Risks:**
1. **Limited TAM:** Soap market is niche (~20K businesses)
   - *Mitigation:* Expand to adjacent markets (candles, cosmetics, herbalism) in Year 2-3

2. **Seasonal Churn:** Some makers only sell during holidays
   - *Mitigation:* Annual billing discount (15%), demonstrate year-round value (tax prep, planning)

3. **Price Sensitivity:** Craft businesses are budget-conscious
   - *Mitigation:* Free tier, 14-day trial, ROI calculator showing time savings

**Competitive Risks:**
1. **SoapMaker Pro Launches Cloud:** Incumbent releases SaaS version
   - *Mitigation:* Move fast (6-12 month head start), build community moat, superior UX

2. **Well-Funded Competitor:** VC-backed startup enters with $5M war chest
   - *Mitigation:* Domain expertise, early customer lock-in, recipe data creates switching costs

**Execution Risks:**
1. **Solo Founder:** Key person risk if you're the only team member
   - *Mitigation:* Hire co-founder/CTO (equity), build advisory board, document processes

2. **Customer Acquisition:** Can't reach target market cost-effectively
   - *Mitigation:* Multi-channel approach (organic + paid + partnerships), iterate quickly

**Technological Risks:**
1. **Supabase Vendor Lock-In:** Platform dependency
   - *Mitigation:* Standard PostgreSQL schema, can migrate to AWS RDS if needed

2. **Security Breach:** Customer data compromise
   - *Mitigation:* SOC 2 compliance prep, security audits, encryption, bug bounty program

**Overall Risk Assessment:** **Medium** (balanced by strong market need, technical execution, and multiple exit paths)
